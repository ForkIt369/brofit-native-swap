Understanding The Limitations

🔒

CORS Restrictions

Cross-Origin Resource Sharing prevents direct DOM access to iframe content. We can't modify the widget's internal styles or structure from outside.

🎨

Container-Level Styling

All CSS approaches work by styling the container, overlays, or surrounding elements. The widget iframe itself remains untouched.

🤝

Partner Solutions

For deep customization (colors, fonts, logo), contact RocketX for a custom subdomain like w3dv-widget.rocketx.exchange with CSS variable overrides.
